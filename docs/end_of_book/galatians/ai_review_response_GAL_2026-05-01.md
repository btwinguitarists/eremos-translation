## Item E — GAL 6:16 "the Israel of God"

**Both AIs FINE.** Both affirm preserving Greek καί-ambiguity by Thai และ. Forcing epexegetic ("namely") or additive ("and also") onto the text would overstep optimal-equivalence into translator-exegesis.

**Disposition:** **LOCK** ambiguous และ + footer note added at 6:16 listing the three-way fork (epexegetic / additive / mixed-eschatological-Israel) for theological-reviewer awareness.
