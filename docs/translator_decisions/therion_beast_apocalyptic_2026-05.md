# θηρίον (apocalyptic Beast) vs ζῷον (heavenly Living-Creature) — tripartite Thai split (LOCKED)

**Status:** **LOCKED 2026-05-04** after end-of-book external sanity-check (ChatGPT + Gemini both verdict FINE; both recommended formalizing this doc).

**Scope:** Revelation's animal vocabulary — preserving the moral / ontological gulf between heavenly attendants of God, eschatological agents of Satan, and generic earth-fauna.

---

## The locked rule

| Greek lemma | Context | Thai | Verses | Register |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **ζῷον** | Heavenly Living-Creatures around God's throne | **สิ่งมีชีวิต** ("living-being") | 4:6, 4:7, 4:8, 4:9, 5:6, 5:8, 5:11, 5:14, 6:1, 6:3, 6:5, 6:6, 6:7, 7:11, 14:3, 15:7, 19:4 | Neutral, attendant-of-God |
| **θηρίον** (apocalyptic) | Sea-beast + earth-beast / False-Prophet of REV 13ff | **สัตว์ร้าย** ("evil-animal" / monster) | 11:7, 13:1, 13:2, 13:3, 13:4, 13:11, 13:12, 13:14, 13:15, 13:17, 13:18, 14:9, 14:11, 15:2, 16:2, 16:10, 16:13, 17:3, 17:7, 17:8, 17:11, 17:12, 17:13, 17:16, 17:17, 18:2, 19:19, 19:20, 20:4, 20:10 | Eschatological-evil, monstrous |
| **θηρίον** (generic fauna) | "Wild-animals-of-the-earth" alongside sword/famine/pestilence | **สัตว์ป่า** ("wild-beast" / fauna) | 6:8 (only) | Natural, non-evil |

---

## §1 — Why three Thai categories, not two

Flat-rendering ζῷον and θηρίον both as **สัตว์** would have collapsed the moral / ontological hierarchy of Revelation's animal-vocabulary:

- The four **ζῷα** at the throne (4:6ff) are faithful **attendants of God** — they sing the Trisagion, lead worship, hand out the seven bowls. Rendering them as **สัตว์ร้าย** would be theologically catastrophic.
- The two **θηρία** of REV 13 are Satan's instruments — the sea-beast (false-Christ) and the earth-beast / False-Prophet. Rendering them as **สัตว์** (neutral) would lose the monstrous-eschatological register.
- The generic θηρίων at 6:8 (with sword/famine/pestilence) is natural fauna — neither attendant-of-God nor Satan's-instrument. Rendering it as **สัตว์ร้าย** would falsely cast natural disasters as eschatological-evil.

The tripartite split tracks the Greek's actual semantic-domain distinctions, not just the lemma surface.

---

## §2 — REV 6:8 — the lemma exception

REV 6:8 is the only Revelation occurrence of θηρίον lemma rendered **สัตว์ป่า** (not **สัตว์ร้าย**). Greek: `καὶ ὑπὸ τῶν θηρίων τῆς γῆς` ("and by the wild-animals of the earth"). Context: fourth-horseman judgment alongside sword (ῥομφαία), famine (λιμός), and pestilence (θάνατος) — all natural means of mortality, not eschatological-evil.

The translator correctly distinguishes context-of-use over lemma-surface. This is the only context-exception; all other θηρίον occurrences (eschatological-Beast contexts, REV 11–20) are **สัตว์ร้าย** uniformly.

---

## §3 — Christological parody at 13:11 (cross-link)

REV 13:11 describes the earth-beast / False-Prophet: `Καὶ εἶδον ἄλλο θηρίον ἀναβαῖνον ἐκ τῆς γῆς, καὶ εἶχεν κέρατα δύο ὅμοια ἀρνίῳ, καὶ ἐλάλει ὡς δράκων`. The Thai uses **สัตว์ร้าย** for the Beast itself + **ลูกแกะ** (NOT พระเมษโปดก) for the lamb-simile — preserving Revelation's wolf-in-sheep's-clothing parody (MAT 7:15). See `docs/translator_decisions/arnion_amnos_lamb_disambiguation_2026-05.md` §3 for the lamb-simile exception.

## Why this matters

- **Worship vs idolatry.** REV 4:8 has the ζῷα singing "ἅγιος ἅγιος ἅγιος" before God's throne; REV 13:8 has the whole earth worshiping the θηρίον. The Thai must not let these two scenes use the same noun.
- **Reader trust.** Thai Christian readers cross-checking with THSV will recognize **สัตว์ร้าย** (monster register) for Beast and **สิ่งมีชีวิต** (neutral) for Living-Creature; the convention is established.
- **Future-proofing.** A future editor may try to "normalize" 6:8's **สัตว์ป่า** to **สัตว์ร้าย** for "consistency" — DO NOT. The 6:8 distinction is principled, tracking Greek-semantic-domain, not lemma-surface.

---

## Alternatives considered

- **Flat สัตว์ for all** — rejected: collapses worship/idolatry distinction.
- **สัตว์ร้าย for both apocalyptic θηρίον AND generic θηρίων at 6:8** — rejected: imposes eschatological-evil register on natural-fauna context.
- **สิ่งมีชีวิต for ζῷον + สัตว์ for all θηρίον (regardless of context)** — rejected: loses the apocalyptic-monstrous register that Revelation's ch. 13ff requires.

---

## When to revisit

- 1 John, James, Jude — minor, but verify any θηρίον usage tracks the same semantic-domain principle.
- Hebrews end-of-book — Hebrews doesn't use ζῷον / θηρίον much, but verify cross-corpus consistency.
- If any future Thai reviewer flags **สัตว์ร้าย** as too-strong (e.g., reads as "horror-fiction" register rather than "biblical-apocalyptic"), revisit with native-speaker intuition; the choice is firmly within standard Thai-Christian convention.
