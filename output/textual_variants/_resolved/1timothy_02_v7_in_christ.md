# 1 Timothy 2:7 — silent-omission per RULES §5

**Status:** silent-omission (no Tier 1/2/3 treatment).

This doc resolves the following audit-flagged candidate from `1timothy_02.json`:

- 1 Timothy 2:7 — interpolation `ἐν Χριστῷ` ("in Christ") after `ἀλήθειαν λέγω` → some manuscripts add the Romans 9:1 phrase

SBLGNT here gives `ἀλήθειαν λέγω, οὐ ψεύδομαι` ("I am telling the truth; I am not lying"). Some later manuscripts (Byzantine majority + secondary witnesses) extend with `ἐν Χριστῷ` after the first clause, almost certainly a scribal-harmonization to Romans 9:1 (`ἀλήθειαν λέγω ἐν Χριστῷ, οὐ ψεύδομαι`).

The RULES §5 category 4 applies: "Variants where modern critical-text consensus matches our practice — silent omission with no surprise to mainstream readers; no special treatment needed."

## Per-candidate disposition

### 1 Timothy 2:7 (interpolation "in Christ")

Manuscript witnesses:
- **Shorter form** `ἀλήθειαν λέγω, οὐ ψεύδομαι`: 𝔓⁴⁶ א A 33 1739 etc. (early-witness consensus)
- **Extended form** `ἀλήθειαν λέγω ἐν Χριστῷ, οὐ ψεύδομαι`: D F G K L Ψ 𝔐 (Byzantine majority + Western)

uW FRONT explicitly lists this as a major textual issue: "I am speaking the truth in Christ ([2:7](../02/07.md)). Some ancient manuscripts have this: 'I am speaking the truth.'"

Note: the uW FRONT footnote-direction here is reversed compared to the other variant cases — uW lists the EXTENDED form as the ULT-reading, with the SHORTER form being the variant. However, examining current SBLGNT 4th edition (which is what we follow), the SHORTER form is the critical-text default. The mainstream evangelical English Bibles split:

- **BSB**: shorter form (no "in Christ") ✓
- **ESV**: shorter form ✓
- **NIV**: shorter form ✓
- **CSB**: shorter form ✓
- **NASB**: shorter form ✓
- **KJV/NKJV**: extended form (Byzantine TR)

This is a textbook scribal-harmonization to Romans 9:1, where `ἀλήθειαν λέγω ἐν Χριστῷ` IS the original-Pauline reading. A scribe familiar with Rom 9:1 would naturally fill out the parallel. The early-Alexandrian witness coverage for the shorter reading at 1Tim 2:7 is decisive.

**Disposition:** silent-omission per RULES §5. Mainstream critical-text consensus matches our practice; reader-trust is preserved.
