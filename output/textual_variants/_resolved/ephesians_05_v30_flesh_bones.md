# Ephesians 5:30 — silent-omission per RULES §5

**Status:** silent-omission (no Tier 1/2/3 treatment).

This doc resolves the following audit-flagged candidate(s) from `ephesians_05.json`:

- Ephesians 5:30 — extension `ἐκ τῆς σαρκὸς αὐτοῦ καὶ ἐκ τῶν ὀστέων αὐτοῦ` ("of his flesh and of his bones") — omitted by SBLGNT, included by some manuscripts as scribal-harmonization to Genesis 2:23 LXX
