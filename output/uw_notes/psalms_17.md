# Psalm 17 Introduction

## Structure and Formatting

Psalm 17 is one of five psalms that are labeled as “prayers.” (See: [[rc://*/tw/dict/bible/other/deliverer]] and [[rc://*/tw/dict/bible/kt/innocent]])

  1. David asks Yahweh to heed his prayer and pleads his innocence (1–5)
  2. David asserts his belief that God will listen to him and answer him and will save everyone who seeks refuge in God (6–7)
  3. David asks Yahweh to protect him (8–9)
  4. David describes his enemies (10–12)
  5. David asks Yahweh to save him by defeating his enemies who are worldly men (13–14)
  6. David’s hope (15)
