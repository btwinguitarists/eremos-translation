## Chapter intro

# Exodus 35 General Notes

## Structure and Formatting

  - v. 1–3: reiteration of Sabbath command
  - v. 4–9: list of materials for donation (See: [Exodus 25:1–7](../25/01.md))
  - v. 10–19: things they need to make for the Dwelling complex
  - v. 20–29: the people bring all the things listed
  - v. 30–[36:1](../36/01.md): appointing of lead craftsmen to build these things

## Translation Issues in This Chapter

  - In the last chapter some repetition of Yahweh’s instructions began, mostly around the covenant commands. Starting in this chapter and even more so in [chapter 36](../36/01.md), the commands regarding the construction of the Dwelling complex are repeated, often word for word, from [chapters 25–31](../25/01.md). In many verses the only difference will be a change of verb form from instruction to completion. As much as possible, these parallels will be pointed out for the translators with links to the previous verses. Translators should refer back to the previously translated verses for consistency and because notes in this section may not cover everything that was covered in the initial instruction section.
  - Many items, materials, and processes for building the Dwelling may be unknown, but should have already been addressed in [chapters 25–31](../25/01.md).
