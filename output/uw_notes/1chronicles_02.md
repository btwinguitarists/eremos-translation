## 1 Chronicles 2:3

**Quote:** Er … Onan … Shelah … Shua … Judah
**Support:** rc://*/ta/man/translate/translate-names
These are the names of men.

**Quote:** who were born to him by Shua’s daughter, a Canaanite woman
**Support:** rc://*/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive
This can be translated in active form. Alternate translation: [his sons whom Shua’s daughter, a Canaanite woman, bore]

**Quote:** Yahweh
This is the name of God that he revealed to his people in the Old Testament. See the translationWord page about Yahweh concerning how to translate this.

**Quote:** in the sight of Yahweh
**Support:** rc://*/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor
The sight of Yahweh represents his judgment or evaluation. Alternate translation: [as Yahweh judged]

**Quote:** Yahweh killed him
**Support:** rc://*/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy
The reader should understand that Yahweh may have had a person kill Er.
