## Chapter intro

# Psalm 14 Introduction

## Structure and Formatting

Psalm 14 is “wisdom psalm” that describes the difference between how righteous people and wicked people live and the difference between what happens to them. See the discussion of the types of psalms in the Introduction to Psalms.

  1. A description of wicked people (1–4) 
  2. God punishes wicked people but helps his own people (5–7)

## Psalms 14:4

**Quote:** הֲ⁠לֹ֥א יָדְעוּ֮ כָּל־פֹּ֪עֲלֵ֫י אָ֥וֶן אֹכְלֵ֣י עַ֭מִּ⁠י אָ֣כְלוּ לֶ֑חֶם
